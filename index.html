What to automate

Not sure where to start? Look for a CROP problem.

The best candidates for automation are low-value, repetitive tasks. If a task checks one of these four boxes, it’s probably costing you more than you think.

C

Cost

A task that costs too much in labour or in tooling fees.

R

Repetition

A manual task performed regularly, in a predictable way.

O

Opportunity

Another task would bring in more for the same hours.

P

Precision

Human errors that slip through and hurt your bottom line.
